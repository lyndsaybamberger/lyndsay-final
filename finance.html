About Chris

Chris Postell was born and raised in Cincinnati, Ohio. Raised by two forward-thinking academics, at a young age Postell attended The Seven Hills School and later the University of Cincinnati. With a passion for community and making a difference, he purused a Bachelors of Political Science and was a leader in Student Government as well as a founder of his own student organization. This later became the creation of something much bigger than himself.

Soon after being admitted to the University of Cincinnati he create the UC League of Leagends club. This quickly sparked his interest in event planning- specifically with esports in mind. In October of 2015 he created his own Company, AllMid LLC and hosted his first live event for over 800 people. For the next 3 years he traveled the Midwest and Country hosting events for gamers of all types.

Fundamental for his passion of community, Postell has been involved in the Event Management industry for over 7 years now. He has organized over 300 events and led planning and managed everything from Weddings to multi-day conventions. Postell also has an interest in financial investing, political activism, and community engagement.
