Past Events

With over seven years of event management under Chris' belt, he has overseen hundreds of events as small as 10 person gatherings to 30,000 attendee multi-day conventions. After creating his esports events company, AllMid LLC, and subsequently selling it, Chris managed events for Winegardner and Hammons Group, a well-known hospitality business in Cincinnati.

One of the largest events that Postell set-up, ran and managed with AllMid was Indy Popcon in 2016. Managing over 30,000 guests in the PanAm Arena in Indianapolis he left a memorable footprint on indypopcon that would last forever.

Another event that Postell managed was GenCon in 2016. With over 100,000 total attendees, this was the largest even that Postell has ever overseen. Managing all PC and Console gaming events at the convention, GenCon went off without a hitch and Postell's team accomodated over 12,000 gamers over the 4 day event.

Going forward Postell oversaw hundreds of events, ranging from intercollegiate tournaments to streaming gaming for prominent fortune 500's such as Microsoft. Postell continued to also take part in large conventions, working highlight indusry trade shows such as PAX East, PAX West, PAX South, and E3.

Going forward Postell oversaw hundreds of events- not just in e-sports- but in almost every arena an event planner can think of. Weddings, reunions, gatherings, and so much more.
