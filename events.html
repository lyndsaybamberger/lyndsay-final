Past Events

With over eight years of event management under Chris' belt, he has overseen events as small as a 10 person gathering up-to a 30,000 multiple day convention. After creating an E-Sports event company and selling it, Chris managed events for a well-known business in Cincinnati.

While working at Winegardner and Hammons Group, Postell was the event manager for prominent locations in Cincinnati and Kentucky. He organized, orchestrated, and managed events of all kinds and sizes for non-profits, corporations, tours, weddings, and sports teams.

Examples of prominent groups that Postell ha*s worked with include: First Church of Christ, Frost Brown Todd, Global Business Travel Association, Newport Aquarium, the Wave Foundation, Proctor & Gamble, Trinity Health Network, Mid America Dart Association, and Kevin O'Leary.

Common event themes included full service catering, AV solutions, housing and hotel agreements, floor plan design, and accomodating special client requests.

Postell is a member of MPI (Meeting Professionals International) in the Cincinnati Chapter and is currently still working with Winegardner & Hammons while purusing a Master's degree at the University of Cincinnati.

One of the first events that Postell set-up, ran and managed with his new born company was Indy Popcon in 2016. Managing thousand of guests constantly coming and going- he left a footprint on indypopcon forever.

Another even that Postell managed was at GenCon in 2016. This was the largest even that Postell has ever overseen and facilited. With multiple formats for gaming, watching and audience participation-GenCon went off without a hitch at the reign of Postell.

Going forward Postell oversaw hundreds of events- not just in esports- but in almost every arena an event planner can think of. Weddings, reunions, gatherings, and so much more.
